Cell style animation

The animation method anime is made with

Cel animation is one of the most traditional forms of animation. It involves parts of the animation (e.g. the foreground, the background, characters...) being hand-drawn onto clear celluloid sheets and layered over each other. The individual sheets are known as animation cels. Animators create animation sequences by drawing each frame of the animation in such a cell, and playing each cell after the other to create a cohesive, moving animation.

Stop motion animation

The most traditional form of animation

Stop-motion animation is the oldest form of animation. It involves photos of characters/backgrounds being taken in sequence, with characters at slightly different positions in each photo. After all the photos are taken, they're assembled together and edited into a cohesive video, so the characters in the images appear to be moving as the frames go on.

3d animation

How disney and pixar's movies are made

3d animation is a style of animation where 3d models made in programs such as maya or blender are "rigged", or animated with code. This form of animation is used in many movies, such as Up, Rapunzel, Jurassic world.... To colour the 3d models, 3d animators apply shaders or textures to the 3d models used.
